Our Story

Empowering Uttarakhand.
Inspiring India.

Growing up in the mountains of Uttarakhand, Harish Singh saw talented young people struggle to access quality education that could change their lives. High fees, distant cities, and complicated jargon kept millions from reaching their potential.

In 2024, he launched Uttarakhand Future Technology with a bold commitment: world-class digital education at prices that everyone can afford. Not as a gimmick — but as a genuine mission.

Today, 12,000+ students from across India are learning, earning, and building careers they once only dreamed of. And we're just getting started.
